# 🚀 Study Zen: Initial Release (v1.0.0)

Welcome to the official launch of **Study Zen**! 🎉

This is our very first major release, bringing a complete, all-in-one productivity and learning ecosystem right to your fingertips. Whether you are studying for exams, learning new skills, or managing your time, Study Zen has everything you need wrapped in a beautiful, distraction-free interface.

## ✨ Epic Features Included

🧠 **AI-Powered Tutor**
- Real-time voice conversations using advanced Speech-to-Text and TTS.
- Intelligent, instant explanations powered by the lightning-fast Groq API.

⏳ **Deep Work & Productivity**
- Built-in Pomodoro Timer to keep you highly focused.
- Beautiful, animated session tracking and study statistics.

📝 **Smart Notes & Quizzes**
- **AI Note Scanning:** Instantly digitize your physical notes!
- Rich Markdown Note Editor with dynamic highlighting.
- Auto-generated Quizzes and Flashcards to test your knowledge retention.

🎮 **Gamified Learning**
- Level up as you learn with our beautiful Progress Dashboard.
- Earn streaks, unlock achievements, and track your daily consistency.

💻 **Built-in Code Compiler**
- Practice your programming skills directly within the app!

🎨 **Stunning UI/UX**
- Gorgeous Glassmorphism design language.
- Fluid Lottie animations and dynamic dark/light theme support.
